# Design Guidelines — Influence Coach

## Brand Identity
- Professional, trustworthy, and warm
- Conveys executive-level credibility without being cold or intimidating
- Feels like a personal coach, not a corporate tool

## Color Palette
- Primary: Deep navy blue (#1A2B4A) — authority, trust, depth
- Accent: Gold/amber (#C9A84C) — excellence, achievement, leadership
- Background: Off-white/light cream (#F8F6F1) — warmth, approachability
- Text: Dark charcoal (#2C2C2C) — readability
- Secondary accent: Soft teal (#4A8FA8) — growth, forward movement

## Typography
- Headings: Serif font (e.g., Playfair Display or Georgia) — gravitas and credibility
- Body/Chat: Clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter or Lato) — readability and modern feel
- Font sizes: Generous, accessible sizing

## Elevation & Depth
- Subtle shadows on chat bubbles and cards
- Clean, uncluttered layout
- Soft borders and rounded corners for approachability

## Components
- Chat interface: Clean message bubbles, coach messages visually distinct from user messages
- Coach avatar/icon: Simple leadership icon or initials badge
- Level indicators: Visual representation of the 5 Levels (progress-style or step indicators)
- CTA buttons: Gold accent, rounded, prominent
- Input field: Clean, spacious, easy to type in

## Overall Feel
- Premium but accessible
- Focused and distraction-free
- Encourages reflection and action

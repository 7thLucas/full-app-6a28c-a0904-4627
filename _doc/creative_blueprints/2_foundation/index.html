Why It Exists

The Problem

Most leaders don't know which level of influence they're operating at — and generic leadership advice rarely meets them where they are. Without a clear diagnosis, well-intentioned coaching falls flat and real growth stalls.

The Solution

A coaching chatbot grounded in Maxwell's 5 Levels of Leadership. It diagnoses first — pinpointing exactly where a leader stands — then delivers concise, empathetic, and actionable guidance to strengthen that level or advance to the next.
